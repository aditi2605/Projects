About

A self-driven strong, imaginative and fashion-conscious girl ready to start her career as an entry-level web developer carrying a very rare set of skills from sales to web-development. Inspired by Mr. Robot and my previous job being a counsellor for students in tech, I always had a natural attraction towards IT especially for creating unique templates that will be used for a wide range of audience to view globally. Having the general knowledge of the elements required in front-end, I finally began my journey in the domain in April 2021. During my learning process, I overcame great difficulties of self-teaching by strengthening my researching skills, setting short goals, constantly practicing by creating and experimenting with new projects. I have gained a short internship from a well-established web development company, giving me a realistic and hands-on exposure to real-world projects that improved my skills from project management and communicating with clients to creating complete website front ends as per the requirement. I am currently on the path of improving my skills in React.js such that I can create more diverse projects such as APIs that will be embedded within a website as well as researching deep into different types of animation that can be created solely using CSS.
